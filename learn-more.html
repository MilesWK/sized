What is sized?

Sized is a simple tool to allow developers to test out there websites on various device widths. Depending on how you style the page, a website could look good for a PC, but when you put it on a phone, the website may become jumbled up and messy. This tool allows you to enter a URL, and see how it will look on devices such as a phone without needing a phone.
